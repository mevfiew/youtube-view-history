# Privacy Policy — YouTube Watch History Pro

**Last updated:** April 17, 2026

## Overview

YouTube Watch History Pro is a browser extension that logs YouTube videos you encounter while browsing youtube.com. Your privacy is fundamental to this extension's design.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, transmit, or share any data.** Period.

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API. No data ever leaves your device.

## What is stored locally

When you browse youtube.com, the extension captures and stores the following information in your browser:

- Video ID and URL
- Video kind (video or short)
- Title
- Channel name and channel ID
- Thumbnail URL
- Duration (as displayed) and duration in seconds
- View count text (e.g., "1.2M views")
- Published time text (e.g., "2 weeks ago")
- Source (home / search / channel / watch-next / shorts / subscriptions)
- Timestamp of when you first viewed it

## Data sharing

None. Zero. There are no servers, no analytics, no third-party services. The extension does not make any network requests of its own.

## Data retention

Data is stored until you delete it. You can:
- Delete individual videos from your history
- Clear all history at once from the extension popup
- Uninstall the extension, which removes all stored data

The extension stores a maximum of 10 000 items. Oldest items are automatically removed when this limit is reached.

## Permissions

- **storage** — To save your watch history locally in Chrome
- **host_permissions (youtube.com / youtu.be)** — To run the content script that captures viewed videos on YouTube
